PEOPLE IN VICTORIA

The estimated amount of people in Victoria is 397,237, 13% of the people are younger then 15, 64% is 15-64, and 23% are seniors. Most of the ethnicity of Victoria is English, Australian, Irish, Indigenous, and a mixture of different Asian ethnicities. The culture is incredibly open in Victoria, allowing lots of different events, art, buildings, stores, music, and food relating to all the diverse cultures they have.

There are around 39,700 professional artists in British Victoria, they take up 20% of the 202,900 artists in Canada. Most of the artists in Victoria are self employed. According to the city of Victoria “creativity is a key to a vibrant community” this is why they support public art so much. They also support and champion local artists through its Indigenous Artist in Residence and Artist in Residence programs.
