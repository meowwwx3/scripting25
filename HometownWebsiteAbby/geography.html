VICTORIAS GEOGRAPHY

Geographically Victoria is an exceptionally beautiful city, it is a mix between mountain terrain and flat land. According to topographic map it can be characterized by coastal cliffs, rolling hills, and lush valleys. Victoria has the mildest winter climate within Canada which allows you to do way more outdoor activities year-round. Victoria is the perfect place to go biking, with many good bike trails and bike accessible city infrastructure, friendly to all riders. As well as their diverse city transportation you can explore the city without the hassle of traffic. From the sky train to the water taxi to the bus system there are many places to go with ease. Even water planes!
